Simple Omelette Recpie

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs
cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  1. Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  2. Preparation: 5 minutes
  3. Cooking: 5 minutes

    4. Ingredients

    1. 2-3 large eggs
    2. Salt, to taste
    3. Pepper, to taste
    4. 1 tablespooon of butter or oil
    5. Opitional fillings:Cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

      6. Instructions

      1. 1.

        Beat the eggs: in a bowl beat eggs with pinch of salt and pepper until they are
        well mixed. you can add tablespoon of water or milk for fluffer texture.

      2. 2.

        Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.

      3. 3.

        Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the
        pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.

      4. 4.

        Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but still are still slightly
        runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.

      5. 5.

        Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully left one edge and fold it
        over the fillings. Lets it cook for another minute, then slide it into a plate.

      6. 6.

        Enjoy: Serve hot, with additonal salt and pepper if needed.
